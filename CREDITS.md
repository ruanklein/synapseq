## Acknowledgements

SynapSeq is inspired by earlier work in the brainwave audio space, particularly tools such as [SBaGen](https://uazu.net/sbagen/), while being a complete rewrite with its own architecture, syntax, and goals.

Third-party libraries and dependencies are credited according to their respective licenses.
